# Usx Stable coin
Here are some of the uses of USX tokens in the sturan network:

### Contribution to the Campaign
USX is used as a stablecoin to make direct contributions to selected campaigns, making the donation process easier.

### Stable Value for Payment
As a stablecoin, USX is designed to maintain a stable value, minimizing the price fluctuations that often occur with cryptocurrencies, making it more suitable for transactions within the sturan network platform.

### Disclaimer
The USX token plays an important role in ensuring stability and convenience in transactions within the platform's ecosystem. Usx is not yet live instead for the initial phase, the sturan network will use usdt or usdc
