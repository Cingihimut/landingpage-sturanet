# Open transaction
All activities related to crowdfunding such as, campaign creation, contribution to the campaign, withdrawal of funds etc. Recorded on the blockchain network, and users can verify each activity without the need for assistance from third parties.

    - ### Full Transparent
        Every transaction made on the platform, from the creation to the closing of a campaign, is accessible to anyone through the blockchain network. This allows users to clearly see the flow of funds and how they are being used. No process is closed, and users do not need to rely on third parties to check the integrity of transactions.
